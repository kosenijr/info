Keyboard: 
Hold Shift and use the left/right arrows to select a text area, 
then press Enter to copy. 
Paste text by pressing Insert.

Mouse: 
Left-click and drag to highlight a text selecting, 
then right click to copy. 
Move the cursor to the desired location and right-click 
to paste the previously copied text.

These commands work with all Windows terminal applications, 
as well as many other operating system terminals.
